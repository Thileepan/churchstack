What makes church to love us

  • Profile Management

    Complete track information about the families including name, address, email, phone number, and dates such as birthday, marriage and track many more additional information.

  • User Management

    Create different login for different users like super admin, manager, financier.

  • Dashboard

    Get know about your church activities from a single place.

  • Subscription Management

    This is a finanical module where you can effectively manage different types of funds which is deposited towards your church.

  • Events/Calendar

    This module helps to schedule the events/meetings within the church more effectively. It also helps to view the list of events in a calendar view.

  • Multiple Settings

    Customize your application in your own way. All module settings can be configured from a single page.

  • Harvest Management

    Again part of the finanical module where you can manage yearly harvesting information. This module will be linked with Subscription Management.

  • Extensive Reports

    Pull your church data as reports as you want. You can download the reports in xls, csv, pdf formats and take print as well.
